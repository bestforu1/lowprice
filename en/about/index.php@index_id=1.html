Business Concept

Flying” refers to butterfly while “Cow” refers to dairy cattle
The standard color for Flying Cow Ranch is the flying cow green mixed by a certain proportion of blue, white and green.
Through the developmental strategies of the three aspects of life (life, production and ecology) and three aspects of education (nourishment, conservation and education), we carry out a recreational business with a sustainable operation and great edutainment to provide our guests with an all rounded leisure experience in terms of body, mind, and soul.
