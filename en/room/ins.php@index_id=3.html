    Campsite Reservation Form
    Campsite Space
    A: With a total area of 8 meters x 8 meters, each campsite space is large enough to accommodate a 6-person tent and a regular car. Those who wish to cook shall make a reservation for an additional campsite space.
    B: Electricity: 100 volts and 15 amperes. Do not use high power consumption equipment to avoid potential dangers and make sure to bring your own power strip with a total length of more than 200 centimeters.
    C: Pavement: open-air & grassland.
    D: With one wash basin and one picnic table.
    E: Campsite maintenance fee includes parking and cleaning fee.
    F: We uphold the campsite operating principle of “first come, first served”.
    Reminder
    A: We uphold the campsite operating principle of “first come, first served”. Campsite space arrangement is to be made after 7:00 am on the day of check-in while tent disassembly is to be done before 11:00 am on the day of check-out.
    B: Please bring your own tent and sleeping bag.
    C: A total of 2 hairdryers are provided in the public shower room of the campsite area.
    D: Light will be cut off in the barbecue area at 23:00 pm; in the campsite area at 23:00 pm; and in the streetlight area at 24:00 pm.
    E: Hot water supply is available in the shower room from 13:00 pm to 24:00 pm. Shower rooms are provided in Area A and Church Plaza.
    F: Since the source of water supply for the camping area and the barbecue area is groundwater, it is not suitable for drinking. Please prepare your own water supply.

    Cancellation
    • Hotel room cancellation based on standard form contract:
      If a tourist cancels a room reservation due to typhoon, earthquake, or other natural disasters, a service charge 10% of the advance deposit shall be deducted with the rest returned to the tourist.
      * If a tourist cancels a room reservation 14 days in advance before check-in (14 days included), a 100% of advance deposit shall be fully returned and a handling charge remittance of NT$30 shall be paid.
      * If a tourist cancels a room reservation 10~13 days in advance before check-in (13 days included), a 70% of advance deposit shall be fully returned and a handling charge remittance of NT$30 shall be paid.
      * If a tourist cancels a room reservation 7~9 days in advance before check-in (9 days included), a 50% of advance deposit shall be fully returned and a handling charge remittance of NT$30 shall be paid.
      *If a tourist cancels a room reservation 4~6 days in advance before check-in (6 days included), a 40% of advance deposit shall be fully returned and a handling charge remittance of NT$30 shall be paid.
      * If a tourist cancels a room reservation 2~3 days in advance before check-in (3 days included), a 20% of advance deposit shall be fully returned and a handling charge remittance of NT$30 shall be paid.
      * If a tourist cancels a room reservation on the day of check-in, no advance deposit shall be returned.
